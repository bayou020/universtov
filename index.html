Individual Patients & Medical Family Office

Universtov specializes in coordination of international medical treatment for individuals seeking care abroad, specifically in Europe and the United States. Also, we offer a unique and innovative “Medical Family Office” tailored to families who wish to prioritize their healthcare.

State & Government Institution

About Us

Universtov is a leader in healthcare management, offering a wide range of services to both individuals and organizations.

We specialize in coordinating all stages of medical journeys abroad and provide a highly personalized healthcare management services for individuals, families, government agencies, insurance companies and corporate employees One of our key offerings is our consulting service, which helps medical institutions implement the latest tools and medical technologies. Our team of experts works closely with hospitals and clinics to identify areas for improvement and develop a customized plan to implement new technologies and improve overall patient care.

What sets us apart from other similar healthcare management services is our team of experienced professors, doctors, nurses, assistants, and other medical experts, who have been carefully selected for their excellence and are affiliated with the world’s best medical institutions in Europe and the United States. .

Finally, Universtov boasts a multicultural front office team, proficient in multiple languages and dedicated to ensuring your health and well-being

Whether you are an individual in need of medical care or a medical institution looking to improve patient outcomes and stay ahead of the curve, Universtov has the expertise and resources to help you achieve your goals. Trust in our excellence, exclusivity, and dedication to your health and well-being.
